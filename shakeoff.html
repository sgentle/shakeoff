Shake-off!

Test your negotiating skills in this realistic business simulator. You need your hand (>) to shake your negotiating partner's hand (<) to close the deal. The closer you are to your end, the more points you get! Be quick, though, because there's a 10 second time-limit before Google buys all your competitors and ruins the deal, harshing your mellow and costing you valuable points.

Controls: arrow keys to move, type and backspace to change your name
Press enter when ready.
